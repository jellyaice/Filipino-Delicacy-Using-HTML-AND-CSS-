DISCOVER PHILIPPINES

Longganisa or Philippine Sausage is said to have come from the Spanish word Longaniza. There are multiple types of Longganisa out there but it basically comes down to just two (2) major categories: the de recado and the hamonado. The de recado is the more savory and salty kind of longganisa while the hamonado is the sweeter version. However, due to the unbeatable creativity of Filipinos, they continuously modified these versions of Longganisa and made them their own.
